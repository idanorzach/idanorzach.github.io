Research

I am currently working with Professor Nati Linial on a bachelor's honors thesis, and we have a paper in work. We are working on bounds on unique-neighbor codes, namely codes that satisfy the unique-neighbor property in the context of expander graphs. Those codes are important as they can and are used to implement efficient decoding algorithms, called message-passing algorithms.
Manuscript: pdf.

I am also working with Professor Omri Weinstein on a thesis project in the area of numerical linear algebra and sketching.
